Rachel Osei-Asamoah's database project:

The functionalities of the Database:

  1. With the use of the website, the user should be able to start and end the application.

  2. The customer (student) registers with a preferred date and receives a notification which acknowledges receipt. After which a call is made after 2-4 days to confirm booking.

  3. This database should track details of customers (students) who have been enrolled in specific schools to improve upon relations with the schools searched for.

  4. The database should be able to compute the highest price of a package at a given time.

  5. The database should keep track of employee details such as full name, password, email and just to mention a few.

  6. The database should keep record of coaches with their various field of expertise.

  7. The database should keep track of customer order details especially the times they registered.

  8. The database should record student details with the various schools they were admitted into and date of placement to record efficiency and success rate of the students.
